About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. The company serves consumers, and small and mid-sized businesses; and the education, enterprise, and government markets. It distributes its products through its retail stores, online stores, and direct sales force; and third-party cellular network carriers, wholesalers, retailers, and resellers.
